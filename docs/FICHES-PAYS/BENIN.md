## 1. Executive Summary

### Snapshot Pays

- **Capitale:** Porto-Novo (official) / Cotonou (de facto business center)
- **Population:** 14 millions
- **GDP:** $18 milliards (1,300 USD/capita)
- **Marché immobilier:** $2-3 milliards/an
- **Système politique:** Démocratie stable (Benin = "Africa's success story" ref. stability)

### Opportunité SafeLand

Le Bénin est priorité AfDB pour la digitalisation foncière (reduction of corruption, FDI catalyst). Le marché immobilier urbain (Cotonou boom) et marché agricole rural offrent opportunité double. SafeLand positionne Bénin comme hub régional (gateway Nigeria, Togo, Niger), établit crédibilité AfDB et prépare scale-out Afrique de l'Ouest.

### Fit SafeLand

- **Avantage concurrentiel:** Stabilité gouvernementale, équipe réformiste MoLU, hub régional potentiel
- **Taille marché:** €170K Year 1, €350K+ Year 2
- **Impact:** 5,000+ titles digitized, FDI confidence signal, regional reference
- **Délai:** 5 mois go-live (octobre 2026)

## 2. Contexte Légal & Réglementaire

### Droit Foncier

**Cadre hérité:**
- Droit foncier français (colonial) = système legacy
- Dual tenure: formal title (urban) + customary (rural)
- Ministry of Land & Urbanism (MoLU) = weak IT infrastructure

**Réformes en cours (2024-2026):**
- Modernization initiative (AfDB-supported)
- Digital cadastre project (slow progress to date)
- SafeLand = accelerant pour cette agenda

### Succession & Héritage

- **Droit chrétien:** Majorité (80%+ population)
- **Droit coutumier:** Dominant zones rurales (customs + Islamic minority)
- **SafeLandFridda:** Multi-system needed (Christian equal distribution vs. customary eldest-male)
- **Adaptateur:** Benin fiche doit inclure local law adapter (rule engine custom)

### Cadre Crypto & Blockchain

- **Politique:** Aucune régulation; tech-friendly government
- **Risk:** Bas (AfDB backing = sufficient validation)

### Conformité & Fiscalité

- **Enregistrement foncier:** 3-5% droits (variable selon type)
- **TVA:** 18%
- **Customs:** Import duties minimal for software
- **Gestion:** Standard (SafeLand collecte + reverse au fisc)

## 4. Roadmap Produit pour le Bénin

### MVP (Octobre 2026) — Urban Pilot + Rural Adapter

**Scope:** 500-1,000 titres (Cotonou urban + Ouémé region rural)

**Modules inclus:**

1. **ERC-721 Land Titles** — Dual formal + customary
   - Urban (Cotonou): formal cadastre data
   - Rural: comité villageois attestations + customary documentation
   - Metadata: owner, surface, location, customary rights notation

2. **Escrow Transactions**
   - Urban: formal notary workflow
   - Rural: village mediator + comité witness
   - Tax integration (Benin fiscal rules)

3. **Succession + Customary Law Adapter**
   - Christian inheritance (equal distribution)
   - Customary Benin (eldest male preference + widow protections)
   - Rule engine: user selects at creation
   - **New module:** Customary law adapter (codify Benin-specific rules)

4. **Tasaluh Pilot** — 50 land disputes (rural mediation)

### Year 1 (H2 2026) — Regional Hub Prep

- Scale to 2,000+ titles (Cotonou + Ouémé)
- Build regional hub capacity (train future partners)
- Module revenue: €40K (customary adapter licensing)

### Year 2 (2027) — Nigeria Gateway

- Scale Benin to 5,000+ titles
- **Nigeria scoping:** Prep for Benin → Nigeria integration
  - Nigerian partners based in Cotonou
  - Use Benin as proof-of-concept
  - Revenue: €100K+ (Nigeria pre-sales, integration)

## 7. Risques & Mitigations

### Risque #1 — AfDB Bureaucratie (Moyen)

**Description:** Slow approval, complex governance, audit requirements.

**Impact:** Funding delays, scope changes.

**Mitigation:**
- Secure first tranche (€100K) before T0
- AfDB pre-approved vendor status
- Monthly reporting to prevent surprises

---

### Risque #2 — Instabilité Régionale (Moyen)

**Description:** Nigeria Boko Haram spillover (Benin north); Benin = buffer zone.

**Impact:** Team security risk, operations disruption.

**Mitigation:**
- Deployment in Cotonou south (low-risk zone)
- No on-ground presence in Benin north
- Monitor security situation quarterly

---

### Risque #3 — Système Coutumier Complexe (Moyen)

**Description:** Multiple ethnic groups (40+), different customary rules per region.

**Impact:** Rule engine doesn't fit all cases, adoption slow in some zones.

**Mitigation:**
- Phase 1: 2-3 major customary systems (60% population coverage)
- Legal review upfront (anthropologist + lawyer consultation)
- Year 2: Expand to additional systems (based on Phase 1 learnings)

---

### Risque #4 — Douanes & Complexité Import (Bas)

**Description:** Software import/licensing regulations unclear.

**Impact:** Licensing disputes, tax complications.

**Mitigation:**
- Legal review (Benin tax counsel)
- Treat SafeLand as "consulting service" (not software product) for tax purposes
- Government MOU clarifies status
