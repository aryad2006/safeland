## 1. Executive Summary

### Snapshot Pays

- **Capitale:** Dakar
- **Population:** 18 millions
- **GDP:** $30 milliards (1,700 USD/capita)
- **Marché foncier:** $3-5 milliards/an (70% rural-agricole)

### Opportunité SafeLand

Le Sénégal est priorité de développement Banque Mondiale (REVE 2020 = réforme foncière majeure). La numérisation des 5+ millions de propriétés foncières (titrage + agricole) déverrouille le crédit rural, finance climatique et intégration financière (80M USD+ potentiel de microcrédits agricoles). SafeLand valide le modèle de financement donateur, établit référence West African, et crée précédent pour autres pays Francophone Afrique.

### Fit SafeLand

- **Avantage concurrentiel:** WB backing, REVE 2020 momentum, dual economy (urban + rural)

- **Impact:** 10K+ titles digitized, 5K+ farmers credit-eligible, €3M+ microcredit unlocked
- **Délai:** 5 mois go-live (août 2026)

## 2. Contexte Légal & Réglementaire

### Droit Foncier & Réforme REVE 2020

**Pre-2020:** Système domanial (collective ownership, état propriétaire de facto)
- Property rights insecure, custom tenure dominant
- No individual titles in rural areas

**REVE 2020 (Réforme réussie, partially implemented):**
- Individual title registration now legal
- Land committees (comités ruraux) legitimized customary rights
- 2-tier system: urban (registry) + rural (committees + optional formal registry)

**Cadre actuel:**
- Ministry of Land & Spaces (weak IT capacity)
- No national cadastre (fragmented by region)
- AGENCE NATIONALE proposed (not yet operational)
- Partnership opportunity = SafeLand fills gap

### Succession & Héritage

- **Droit islamique:** Hanafite + Malékite schools (mixed population)
- **Droit chrétien coutumier:** Majority in rural south (Casamance region)
- **SafeLandFridda:** Multi-school support needed (Hanafite ≠ Malékite distribution rules)
- **Complexity:** Higher than Morocco (dual system), mitigation = rule engine + local legal review

### Cadre Crypto & Blockchain

- **Politique:** Aucune régulation explicite
- **WB blessing:** Green light = sufficient (donateur influence)
- **Risque:** Bas à moyen (WB partnership = protection)

### Conformité & Fiscalité

- **CGI (Code Général des Impôts):** 6% rights of registration (land transfers)
- **TPS:** 18% TVA applicable
- **Land registration fees:** Varient (comités ruraux = informal)
- **Gestion:** SafeLand collecte, reverse au fisc (standard audit model)

## 4. Roadmap Produit pour le Sénégal

### MVP (Août 2026) — Dual Urban + Rural Pilot

**Scope:** 1,000-2,000 titres (Dakar urban + Kaolack rural)

**Modules inclus:**

1. **ERC-721 Land Titles** — Urban registry + rural custom
   - Urban: formal cadastre data (Dakar area)
   - Rural: comité rural attestations + custom tenure documentation
   - Dual metadata schema (formal ≠ custom)

2. **Escrow Transactions** — Buyer-seller-notary
   - Dakar urban: formal notary workflow
   - Rural: local mediator + comité rural witness
   - Tax integration (CGI 6% + TPS)

3. **Succession (Hanafite + Malékite hybrid)**
   - Hanafite: 1/3 épouse, 2/3 enfants (equal distribution children)
   - Malékite: 1/4 épouse, 3/4 enfants (weighted to eldest male)
   - Rule engine: user selects school at inheritance creation
   - Governance: heirs vote for estate manager

4. **Tasaluh Pilot** — Land dispute resolution, 100 cases
   - Rural focus (customary law mediators)
   - UN/UNCDF potential for formalization
   - Blockchain record = proof in government courts

### Year 1 (H2 2026) — AgricBank Integration Start

- Scale to 5,000 titles (rural expansion)
- **AgricBank phase 1:** Manual linking (farmer owns title → eligible for credit)
  - Farmer presents SafeLand title to AgricBank
  - Loan officer verifies on-chain
  - Credit decision faster (3 days vs. 30 days)
- Revenue: €385K (WB + operations)

### Year 2 (2027) — API Integration & Rural Financial Services

- **AgricBank phase 2:** Automated API (SafeLand → AgricBank credit scoring)
  - Real-time collateral valuation
  - Microcredit decisioning (50% faster)
- **Refugees module:** Displaced persons land recovery (UNHCR partnership)
  - 1,000+ IDP cases (Mauritania border spillover)
  - Land restitution tracking post-return
  - Revenue: €100K (if UNHCR funds)
- **Total Year 2:** €600K+

### Year 3 (2028+) — Mobile Offline Mode

- Rural areas poor connectivity (offline-first app)
- Batch sync when online
- Enable 100K+ rural users without broadband

## 7. Risques & Mitigations

### Risque #1 — Bureaucratie WB (Moyen-Haut)

**Description:** WB disbursement slow, complex procurement rules, audit-heavy.

**Impact:** Funding delays, scope creep, schedule slip.

**Mitigation:**
- Secure first tranche (€100K) before T0 (Feb 2026)
- WB procurement pre-approved (SafeLand on WB vendor list)
- Monthly WB reporting (prevents surprises)
- Escalation: PM + WB resident mission director (fast-track if blocked)

---

### Risque #2 — Pénurie Développeurs Locaux (Moyen)

**Description:** Dakar tech ecosystem small, competition high (Orange, SeneGence, etc.).

**Impact:** Hiring delay, cost inflation, quality risk.

**Mitigation:**
- Hire 1 remote immediately (no wait)
- Local junior + mentoring (6-month ramp-up)
- Partner with local tech school (CTIC Dakar) for training
- Retention bonus (SafeLand commitment = 1-year stability)

---

### Risque #3 — AgricBank Legacy Integration (Moyen)

**Description:** AgricBank uses legacy systems (mainframe), no modern API.

**Impact:** Manual integration only (Year 1), delayed revenue from automation.

**Mitigation:**
- Phase 1 manual (farmer presents title, officer checks on-chain)
- Phase 2 API (Year 2, negotiate with AgricBank IT)
- Microfinance backup: work with MFI network if AgricBank stalls
- Revenue still strong (manual title verification = faster loans)

---

### Risque #4 — Refugee Module Uncertainty (Bas)

**Description:** UNHCR funding not confirmed; Mauritania border situation fluid.

**Impact:** Module doesn't launch; revenue miss.

**Mitigation:**
- Year 2 module only (not Year 1 critical path)
- Parallel discussions with UNHCR (separate funding)
- Proof-of-concept with 50 cases (internal funding, 2027)
- Pivot to IDP domestic recovery (internal displacement ongoing)

---

### Risque #5 — Transition Gouvernementale 2027 (Moyen)

**Description:** Elections 2024 → 2027 (next cycle); government change = policy reset.

**Impact:** Ministry champion leaves, buy-in lost, budget cuts.

**Mitigation:**
- Embed SafeLand in ministry operations (make it indispensable)
- Multi-year government budget commitment (signed Year 1)
- WB backing = ministry compliance (donateur influence)
- Bi-partisan framing (land titling = shared goal)
